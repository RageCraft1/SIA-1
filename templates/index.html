About Us

CO*RE also known as Conference Reservation was created on January something of 2022. It was created with the purpose of assisting the user in creating a reservation for a room through online means. It has a function of being able to add a reserved room, view the said reserved room, edit the said reserved room, delete the said reserved room, and get notifications from particpants wanting to join the reserved room and lastly get a notification or an alarm for when the schedulle is almost up.

CO*RE was founded by three talented individuals who are still students of Cebu Institute of Technology. The members are namely Riel Jasper Abangan, Almira Legaspina, and Hannah Mhae Laquinon all majoring in Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.
